Grilled Chicken with Tomatoes and Artichokes

What's great about this recipe

  • Quick and easy – Comes together fast, making it ideal for busy weeknights.
  • Fresh take on chicken – A flavorful change of pace for chicken breasts with simple ingredients.
  • Reheats well – Perfect for leftovers or meal prep throughout the week.
  • Flexible sides – Pairs well with rice, pasta, or bread for a rounded meal.

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and black pepper for the marinade.
  3. Place chicken breasts in a zip-top bag, pour in the marinade, seal, and refrigerate for at least 15 minutes (up to 1 hour).
  4. While the chicken is marinating, prepare the vegetables (dice the tomatoes and drain the artichokes).
  5. Remove chicken from the bag, shaking off excess marinade, and place on the preheated grill. Cook for about 6–8 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F.
  6. In the final 5 minutes of cooking, add sliced tomatoes and chopped artichokes to the grill to warm and slightly char them.
  7. Serve the chicken topped with the warm tomatoes and artichokes for a colorful, delicious presentation!

Substitutions and Variations

  • Herbs – Use 1¼ tsp of Italian seasoning if you don’t have individual dried herbs. Alternatively, use freshly chopped herbs for an aromatic boost.
  • Oil – Olive oil can be replaced with avocado oil or ghee for saut√©ing.
  • Dairy-Free – Omit the butter for a still delicious but lighter sauce option.
  • Tomato-Free – Skip the tomatoes for a lemon–artichoke sauce. Add slices of zucchini or summer squash for color and texture.
